Part 1: A Surprise in Switzerland Today, Switzerland is famous for chocolate. Many people think chocolate was invented there. However, chocolate came from Central and South America. In 1879, he invented a machine called the conche. The machine slowly mixed and stirred chocolate for many hours. This process is called conching. Conching made chocolate smoother, softer, and richer in flavor. It also helped remove unwanted bitterness. People loved the new texture. Chocolate became much more enjoyable to eat. This invention changed chocolate-making around the world.
